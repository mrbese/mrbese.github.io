#ApplyingInPublic

While everyone is building in public, I am JOB HUNTING in public.

This page is the home for the search: what I’m chasing, how I diligence it, and—when I publish them—write-ups per company/role. It is intentionally separate from the homepage so strangers meet proof first, narrative second.

“I am looking for a place that I failed to create for myself as a founder, where I am surrounded by challenging minds who share the same enthusiasm, hunger, and a bit of frustration to dismantle the things that aren’t working and build the things that do.”
What you get here

Same bar as my market research—applied to hiring

  • Thesis — why the company, why now, why me.
  • Diligence — primary sources where it matters (filings, tariffs, specs), not recycled blog takes.
  • Week one — what I would actually do in the first 30 days if I landed the seat.
  • Honest status — applied, passed, ghosted, advanced—labeled plainly when I post an entry.
Per entry

What each public write-up is built to include

Context

Category, geography, regulatory environment, and the specific wedge I believe in.

Role fit

What the job actually is vs what the JD pretends it is—and where I overlap.

Numbers

When possible: unit economics, throughput, risk—same instinct as my deep dives.

Artifact

Links to anything I ship alongside the app (calcs, memos, tools) when relevant.

Proof of depth

This is not vapor “thought leadership”

The Base Power work on my main site is not a job application—it is the same diligence muscle: filings, market structure, hardware cost walk, and an operator take on what I would do next.

If you want to calibrate how I think before any application post exists, start there.
